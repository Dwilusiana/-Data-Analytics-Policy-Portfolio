# Public Perception Analysis on Economic Issues

## Background
Public perception plays an important role in shaping expectations and responses to economic developments. 
Narratives circulating in media and social platforms can influence confidence, risk perception, and decision-making behavior among stakeholders.

## Objective
This project aims to analyze public sentiment toward selected economic issues using publicly available text data, in order to identify dominant narratives and changes in perception over time.

## Research Questions
1. What is the overall sentiment surrounding key economic issues?
2. How does public sentiment change across different periods?
3. What dominant themes and narratives emerge from public discussions?
