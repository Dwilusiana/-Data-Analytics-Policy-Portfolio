# Forecasting Economic Indicator Trends

## Background
Economic indicators are closely monitored to assess stability and potential risks in the economy.
Forecasting key indicators can support early identification of deviations and help inform timely decision-making.

## Objective
This project aims to forecast trends of a selected economic indicator using historical time series data,
with a focus on understanding patterns, uncertainty, and potential early warning signals.

## Research Questions
1. What historical patterns and seasonality are present in the selected economic indicator?
2. How does the forecasted trend evolve in the near term?
3. What level of uncertainty should be considered in interpreting the forecast results?
